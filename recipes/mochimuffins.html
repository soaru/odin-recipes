Mochi Muffins

Description

These yummy mochi muffins made with rice flour and coconut milk make a great breakfast, snack, or casual dessert.

Ingredients

  • Sweet rice flour
  • Sugars
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Coconut milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Vanilla
  • Maple syrup
  • Sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Whisk the dry ingredients together in one bowl and the wet ingredients in another.
  2. Add the dry mixture to the wet mixture.
  3. Spoon the batter into prepared muffin cups and sprinkle with sesame seeds.
  4. Bake the muffins until a toothpick comes out clean.
